Welcome to {Coding Pups} =)

Who We Are?

We are a budding non-profit organization dedicated to educating children from low income communities on how to code, or gain interest in coding.

What we do?

We provide children with the opportunities (during, and after school) to learn code from experienced programmers. We help them gain interest, and build confidence through fun techniques, plus activities.

Where we do it?

We work in schools, community centers, and public libraries all around the NYC area.

Why We Matter?

Coding helps build critical thinking and problem-solving skills. It equips children with valuable technical skills, which will allow them to excel in their future as they grow into young professionals in this new digital age.
